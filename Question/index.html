  • What is the capital city of Australia?
    The capital city of Australia is Canberra. Canberra is located in the southeastern part of the country and serves as the political and administrative center of Australia. It was purpose-built as the capital in the early 20th century and is home to important government buildings, including the Parliament House.

  • Who is the author of the famous novel "To Kill a Mockingbird"?
    The author of the famous novel "To Kill a Mockingbird" is Harper Lee. The novel was published in 1960 and became an instant classic. It addresses themes of racial injustice, moral growth, and the loss of innocence, and it has had a significant impact on literature and discussions about civil rights. Harper Lee's work continues to be widely read and studied in schools and universities worldwide.
